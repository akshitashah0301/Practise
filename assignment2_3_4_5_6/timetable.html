Schedule for kids

A daily schedule in a playhouse, like a classroom or daycare, is crucial for fostering a sense of security, predictability, and learning in young children. It helps them develop essential life skills, manage emotions, and engage more effectively in learning activities.

  • 10:45am to 11:00am
